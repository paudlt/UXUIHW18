Hey there, I’m

Paulina

an Industrial Designer with a passion for products and UX/UI.

Hello! I'm Paulina, an Industrial Designer freshly graduated from CSU Long Beach. Currently immersed in a UX/UI design Boot Camp at UC Berkeley Extension, I'm all about embracing new experiences. My creative journey led me to Hongik University in South Korea, where I absorbed rich design culture. I'm driven by crafting memorable products, blending problem-solving and rapid prototyping with tools like Solidworks, Keyshot, Adobe Suite, Figma, and user-centric design research. My knack for seamless user experiences spans digital platforms and tangible items, including toys, furniture and children and women wellness products. Entrepreneurial at heart, I founded Nikté in 2016 and co-founded La Catrina Mexican Grill in 2014, where I elevated brand identity and sales through innovative design-driven strategies. My passion lies in engineering interfaces and products that spark joy, backed by skills in prototyping, 3D CAD, rendering, user research, usability and A/B testing, and interaction design. A tech enthusiast and collaborative team player. I'm fluent in English and Spanish, bridging communication gaps effortlessly.
